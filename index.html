UNDERSTANDING VR & AR

Virtual Reality(VR)
Immerses users in a fully digital environment using headets like the Oculus Quest and HTC Vive, enabling unparalleed interaction with a computer-generated world.s

Augmented Reality(AR)
Enhances real-world environments by overlaying digital content through smartphones, AR glasses, and future-ready technologies like AR contact lenses

HAPTIC FEEDBACKS

Wearable devices providing physical sensations, enhancing realism and immersion.

Teslasuit

2020

Wearable Haptic Suits

A full-body haptic suit that provides tactics feedback, allowing users to feel sensations like touch, temperature, and impact in VR.

HaptX Gloves

2021

Haptic Gloves

Gloves that use micro-fluidic technology to deliver realistic touch sensation which enables users to interact with virtual objects as if they were real.

Ultrahaptics

2019

Mid-Air Haptics

Uses ultrasonic waves to create tactile sensations in mid-air, allowing users to "feel" virtual objects without physical contact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ask away.

What is the difference between VA and AR?

VR is about escaping reality and immersing users in a completely virtual world. AR is about enhancing reality by adding digital elements to the real world.

Which one is better?

It depends on your specific goal and target audience. VR is better for fully immersive and controlled environment while, AR is better for enhancing real-world experiences with digital overlays..
